Architectural Design Project

Residential Building Plans

Project Overview

This project involved creating detailed architectural plans for a modern residential building in Cavite, Philippines. The design focused on maximizing natural light while maintaining privacy in an urban setting.

Project Details

  • Client: Private Homeowner
  • Location: Imus, Cavite
  • Year: 2024
  • Tools Used: AutoCAD, SketchUp, Lumion
  • My Role: Lead CAD Operator

Design Process

1. Client Consultation

Met with the client to understand their needs, preferences, and budget constraints.

2. Site Analysis

Conducted measurements and analyzed the building site's conditions and constraints.

3. Conceptual Design

Created initial sketches and 3D models to explore design options.

4. Technical Drawings

Developed detailed architectural plans including floor plans, elevations, and sections.

5. Client Presentation

Prepared presentation materials and walked the client through the design.

6. Final Deliverables

Produced complete construction documents for permitting and building.

Challenges & Solutions

Limited Space

The building site was relatively small for the client's requirements. I optimized the layout using space-saving techniques and multi-functional areas.

Natural Light Requirements

The client wanted abundant natural light while maintaining privacy from neighboring buildings. I designed strategic window placements and light wells.
